Blueberry Nuwara Eliya

Spend your vacation in Nuwara Eliya at Blueberry, one of the most luxurious and greatest locations to stay in Nuwara Eliya. Our property provides stylish decor, immaculate rooms, friendly service, and stunning views, assuring you enjoy the most memorable holiday possible. Complete with a sparkling pool overlooking the beautiful lake, and mountain range. Leisurely surveys of green tea plantations, a visit to a tea factory, a mountain trek, are all pastimes that await you when you spend a holiday with a difference. If fun and relaxation is what you seek the doors at The Blueberry are always open to welcome you.
