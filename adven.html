Welcome to Blueberry Adventures

Surfing

Experience an unforgettable surf adventure at the Blueberry, known for its uncrowded, consistent, and perfect waves. Experience the exhilaration of surfing these incredible waves firsthand at Blueberry Colombo.


Sigiriya


Discover the ancient marvel of Sigiriya with our hotel adventures. Unveil the secrets of the iconic 'Lion Rock' a UNESCO World Heritage Site, rising majestically above lush forests and gardens

Boat ride-Kandalama lake


We offer you the chance to embark on a scenic boat ride across this serene lake, surrounded by lush greenery and the ancient echoes of Dambulla's rich history. Glide along the calm waters.

Horton Plains


Horton Plains, Sri Lanka's only national park declared a World Heritage Site, is an eerie highland plateau popular with hikers. A circular trail of 10 km length winds through cloud forests and open grasslands.This is a trek that is more accessible than some, due to the well-maintained paths and the slightly less strenuous hill walking.


Waterfall adventure


Experience the enchanting beauty of Lovers Leap Waterfall in Nuwara Eliya. Nestled amidst the lush, emerald-green tea plantations of Sri Lanka's hill country, this picturesque waterfall is a sight to behold.
